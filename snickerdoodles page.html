Snickerdoodles

Snickerdoodles are really soft and delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 C shortning
  • 1 1/2 C sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 3/4 C flour
  • 2 t cream of tartar
  • 1 t soda
  • 1/2 t salt

sugar mix

  • 1/2 C sugar
  • 2 t cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Cream the shortning, sugar, and eggs together
  2. Sift the flour, tartar, soda, and salt together
  3. Combine both mixtures and roll into 1 inch balls
  4. Roll balls in sugar mix then set on pan
  5. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  6. Put the pan with cookies in oven for 8-10 mins
  7. Then mash them all into a pile and cram them down your throat while they're still piping hot so it burns your throat and causes indigestion, it really brings out the flavor
